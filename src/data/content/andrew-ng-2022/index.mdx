> To me, there's so many things that the world used to do in 2022, they just do not work in 2025. So in fact, often I ask myself, is there anything we're doing that today that we were also doing in 2022? And if so, let's take a look and see if it still even makes sense today because a lot of stuff, a lot of workflows in 2023 don't make sense today. So I think today, the technology is moving so fast 
> 
> ...
> 
> I think it's a challenge for a lot of people. I know many great leaders in lots of different functions still doing things the way they were in 2022. And I think it's just got to change. When new technology comes, I mean, once upon a time, there was no such thing as web search today. Would you hire anyone for any role that doesn't know how to search the web, right? And I think we're well past the point that for a lot of job roles, if you can't use LLMs in an effective way, you're just much less effective than someone that can.

--- [Andrew Ng](https://www.andrewng.org), <a href="https://share.snipd.com/snip/1ce715fe-1874-4396-815b-977499c24b7d" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">No Priors Podcast</a>
